Places to Visit

Siddheshwar Temple

One of the most important spiritual landmarks in Solapur. Dedicated to Lord Siddheshwar and located near Siddheshwar Lake.

Pandharpur

Located 70 km away, famous for Vitthal Rukmini Temple and Wari pilgrimage.

Akkalkot

Located 40 km away, known for Shri Swami Samarth Maharaj temple.

Solapur Fort

Historic Bhuikot Fort built during Bahmani Sultanate period.
